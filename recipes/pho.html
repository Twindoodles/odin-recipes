How to make a bowl of pho

While traditionally the dish needs to simmer for at least 6 hours, we'll make a version that can be done in just an hour to fit in our busy lives, all while still retaining the rich, savory flavors of pho.

Ingredients

  • 1-3 kg/ 2-6 lb of beef bones
  • 800g beef
  • 3 onions
  • fish sauce, salt, sugar, stock powder
  • 2 knobs of ginger
  • 3 star anise
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 2-3 black cardamom
  • 1 tsp cloves (optional)
  • 1 tsp coriander seeds (optional)
  • 1 kg dried rice/ pho noodles
  • 100g spring onion
  • fresh herbs (asian basil, sawtooth herbs)
  • 1 lime
  • 500g bean sprouts
  • Hoisin sauce, Sriracha (optional)
